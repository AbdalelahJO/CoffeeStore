Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of coffee do you sell?

    We offer a selection of 8 premium coffee varieties to suit different tastes. Our range includes black coffee,cappuccino,cortado...and more.

  • Do you offer whole bean or ground coffee?

    We provide both whole bean and ground coffee options for our customers. You can choose your preferred type when placing an order.

  • What roast levels do you offer?

    Our coffee comes in various roast levels, from light to dark. We offer from 1 to 10.

  • How should I store your coffee to keep it fresh?

    To maintain the freshness of your coffee, store it in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight and moisture.

  • What brewing methods are suitable for your coffee?

    Our coffee is versatile and can be brewed using methods such as pour-over, French press, espresso machine, and drip coffee makers. We recommend experimenting to find your perfect cup.

  • Do you offer decaffeinated coffee options?

    Yes, we have decaffeinated coffee available for those who prefer to enjoy coffee without the caffeine.

  • Can I purchase your coffee as a gift for someone?

    Absolutely! You can purchase our coffee as a gift and provide the recipient's shipping address during the checkout process. We can even include a personalized message with the gift.

  • What is your shipping policy?

    We offer shipping to every country in the world except the israel-occupied territories . For details on shipping rates and estimated delivery times, please visit our Shipping page.

  • What is your return and refund policy?

    We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you encounter any issues, please visit our Returns and Refunds page for information on how to request a return or refund.

  • How can I contact your customer support team?

    You can reach our customer support team by Email. We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns.

  • Are your coffee beans ethically sourced?

    We take pride in sourcing our coffee beans from ethical and sustainable producers. Visit our Sustainability page to learn more about our commitment to responsible sourcing.

  • Do you offer subscription services for regular coffee delivery?

    Yes, we offer subscription services that allow you to receive your favorite coffee on a regular basis. Check out our Subscription page for more details.
